Services


What does Pediatric Service provide?

Pediatric Service is a primary care pediatrics practice, our goal is to prevent illness and achieve wellness for your child or adolescent. We provide a full range of pediatric services, including:


  1. Well child exams

  2. Sports physicals/screening tests

  3. Developmental screenings for Early Identification of Developmental Delays & Autistic spectrum disorders

  4. Hearing and Vision Screen

  5. Immunizations

  6. Antibiotic injections for children unable to tolerate oral medications

  7. In-office inhalation treatment for children with severe asthma attacks or wheezing

  8. Evaluation of school failure

  9. Treatment of attention deficit disorder

  10. Tympanometry to check for middle ear fluid

  11. Cryosurgery for treatment of warts & other skin lesions

  12. Coordination of care with specialist

  13. Management of acute illness

What type of labs does Pediatric Service provide in In-office

Pediatric Service has the following on-site lab services available:

  1. CBC

  2. Influenza A & B

  3. Rapid Strep

  4. Lead Screening (sent out)

  5. Dipstick UA

  6. AccuChek

  7. Urine pregnancy test
